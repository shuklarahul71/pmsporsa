About Us

The world has changed enormously since The PMS Porsa welcomed its first students in 2008, but its mission to attract and develop exceptional boys and teachers from all backgrounds to serve a meritocratic India remains as vital now as it was then. With scholarships and bursaries available to support almost half our students, we aim to reduce, as far as possible, any financial barriers to admission.

At The PMS Porsa we take our responsibility to find what is exceptional in boys very seriously. The experience that we have prepared for them here gives every boy an all-round education built on a foundation of Service, Adventure, Sports, Creative Arts and Academics.

As a fully residential school we also nurture a commitment to compassion, respect and well being that we believe is an antidote to the entitlement, anxiety and lack of connection that dominate the day-to-day lives of so many teenagers.

Our experienced and dedicated teachers live, work and play with our boys in Porsa dist Morena (M.P.) . To prepare boys to enter a competitive and interconnected world we build their capacity to collaborate, take intellectual risks and turn their ideas and passions into something original and valuable. We want them to leave The PMS Porsa ready to engage with and contribute to any team they find themselves a member of; life is a team sport after all.

For many years our boys have achieved significantly above national and international averages for MP Board and CBSE results and have been offered places at some of Indiaâ€™s most interesting and selective universities.
