    About Us

    The world has changed enormously since The PMS Porsa welcomed its first students in 2008, but its mission to attract and develop exceptional boys and teachers from all backgrounds to serve a meritocratic India remains as vital now as it was then. With scholarships and bursaries available to support almost half our students, we aim to reduce, as far as possible, any financial barriers to admission. At The PMS Porsa we take our responsibility to find what is exceptional in boys very seriously. The experience that we have prepared for them here gives every boy an all-round education built on a foundation of Service, Adventure, Sports, Creative Arts and Academics.

    For many years our boys have achieved significantly above national and international averages for MP Board and CBSE results and have been offered places at some of India’s most interesting and selective universities.
